About Us

Gobischolars is a global education-driven initiative dedicated to transforming access to quality education. We empower students through individualized mentorship, connect aspiring scholars with prestigious global institutions, and lead the charge in advancing educational reforms through innovative programs. Our mission extends beyond borders, shaping future leaders and fostering a global exchange of knowledge and ideas.

Our Story

Growing up as a nomad in eastern Mongolia, Bayarjavkhlan (Jack) Bayanmunkh experienced the scarcity of educational resources and guidance. Motivated by this experience, he co-founded Gobi Scholars to create a supportive community of scholars committed to shaping Mongolia's future. Gobi Scholars connects ambitious Mongolian students with top U.S. colleges and offers opportunities for U.S. students to teach in Mongolia, bridging global knowledge with local needs. In addition to empowering students, we organize educational forums to engage policymakers, raise awareness about the challenges facing Mongolia's education system, and advocate for meaningful reforms. By uniting students, educators, and policymakers, Gobi Scholars is dedicated to building a stronger, more innovative education system for Mongolia's future.
