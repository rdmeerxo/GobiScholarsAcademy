First Ever Gobischolars Internship Program

Gobischolars is a dynamic education-focused NGO working to transform lives through learning. Our mission is simple: to empower the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and innovators. With initiatives like personalized college prep, groundbreaking research, and international fellowships, we're creating a global community of changemakers.

Our Students

Every year, Gobischolars selects 12 talented and driven high school students to join our comprehensive college preparation program. Through 100% financial aid, we provide these students with personalized guidance, mentorship, and resources to navigate the college application process and achieve their academic dreams.

Meet our students and discover their inspiring journeys.
